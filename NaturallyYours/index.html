Naturally Yours

Naturally Yours is an independently owned eco-emporium located in downtown historic Jim Thorpe in the Pocono Mountains of Pennsylvania. Since opening in August of 2007 we have provided our customers with eco-conscious alternative products for everyday use. We endeavor to carry products that have as low an impact on our environment and our health as possible. With that goal in mind, we carry organic cotton, bamboo, alpaca and hemp clothing for adults and infants. We also carry honey and beeswax based soaps, natural personal care items, essential oils, massage oils and stainless steel bottles. We attempt to obtain as much of our product as close to our business as is possible. As such, we have local artists supply clothing accessories and jewelry from recycled materials. We also carry fair trade clothing, accessories, jewelry and handcrafted "treeless" paper products.
